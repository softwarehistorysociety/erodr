erodr is the brainchild of Drew Halliday. He came up with the idea while attending the University of Missouri, Columbia (Mizzou). In early 2012 he began to develop prototypes for a student-designed local social network with better privacy than other social media. Importantly, this network would create a community of students around the campus where users could meet people not already friends in their existing social media. Version 1 was launched on the App Store on November 18, 2012, in a closed beta at Mizzou.
