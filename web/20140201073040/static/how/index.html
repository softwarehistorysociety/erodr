erodr is private mobile social networking for the local college student community.

It creates a geo-fenced social Forum around the local campus. For the first time since Facebook was college-students only, erodr provides an open social platform for the student community that is private from the rest of the world. On erodr you can meet new students around you. 

Validate your student email address to see posts to erodr by other students around campus
When you post in the local forum, it is seen by other students nearby not already in your other social networks.  Those students can leave private likes and comments, remarks seen only by you, which is how other students introduce themselves if interested in you or what you post. You can ignore or block them, or request to Connect to enable 1:1 private messaging.

erodr has better privacy. 
  1. You are invisible until you like someone's post or comment, and only they can see that you did...or post something yourself to appear publicly and collect likes and comments. 
  2. All posts to the erodr streamer expire in hours, and aren't kept on our servers after expiration.   
  3. You can post with limits on duration (down to 30 minutes), range (down to 100 feet), and limit the audience (connections only, men or women only).
  4. Users can post anonymously once every other day. 
 
erodr is community moderated: likes, dislikes, and flags determine each post's duration and viewable range. Posts can expire in minutes if disliked by other students.

erodr is an addictive hyperlocal crowd-sourced entertainment feed where the student community mingles and meets. 

With your Connections on erodr, you can use our Radar feature, which lets you share your current location on campus with Connections only, and only when each of you has turned Radar ON.
